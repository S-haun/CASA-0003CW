Net Zero Emission Target

Introduction

In June 2019, the UK government legislated a net zero emissions target by 2050. In 2021, the government set two additional interim targets to run a net zero power system and reduce emissions by 78% by 2035.

Discussion

Currently: The UK's Zero Net Emissions policy got off to a late start, but some changes can be seen after the policy was published (from 2019).

Electricity generated from renewable energy has grown steadily, but other factors have not developed as much. Overall, there is still a long way from the target.

Advice: increased use of electrical transport, popularize more efficient heaters, use more thermally friendly building materials.
